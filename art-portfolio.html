Behind The Scenes

My Creative Process

01

Inspiration & Concept

Every artwork begins with inspiration. I gather ideas from nature, urban environments, emotions, and personal experiences. This stage involves research, sketching initial concepts, and exploring different directions.

02

Preliminary Sketches

Once I have a clear concept, I create preliminary sketches to explore composition, form, and structure. This stage helps me visualize the final piece and work through any technical challenges.

03

Medium Selection

I select the appropriate medium based on the concept and desired effect. Whether it's graphite for detailed sketches, watercolor for fluid expressions, or mixed media for textural complexity, each medium offers unique possibilities.

04

Creation & Refinement

The creation process involves building layers, adding details, and continuously refining the artwork. I work iteratively, stepping back regularly to assess the piece and make adjustments as needed.

05

Completion & Reflection

Once the artwork is complete, I take time to reflect on the process and outcome. This reflection informs future projects and contributes to my ongoing artistic development.
